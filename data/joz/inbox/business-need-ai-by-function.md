The strongest way to extend Joz for business value is by mapping AI opportunities by business function rather than by generic AI category.

Core function areas:

1. Finance and accounting
- invoice handling
- AP and AR workflows
- close support
- spend visibility
- forecasting support
- anomaly detection

2. ERP and operations
- procurement
- exception handling
- inventory visibility
- planning support
- supplier coordination
- fulfillment bottleneck analysis

3. HR and workforce
- recruiting support
- onboarding
- policy Q&A
- manager support
- internal service workflows
- training and knowledge reuse

4. Marketing
- campaign planning
- reporting synthesis
- segmentation
- content operations
- attribution interpretation
- CAC efficiency support

5. Sales and revenue operations
- lead qualification
- proposal and follow-up support
- CRM hygiene
- forecasting assistance
- churn and renewal signals
- next-best action

6. Leadership and transformation
- cross-functional reporting
- operating model redesign
- KPI synthesis
- scenario support
- AI adoption governance

This is useful because leaders usually buy outcomes by function, not abstract agent categories.
