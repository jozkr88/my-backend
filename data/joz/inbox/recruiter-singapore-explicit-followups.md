Recruiter Singapore Explicit Follow Ups

Purpose
This record covers the follow-up questions that should only use Singapore-specific framing when the recruiter explicitly asks for Singapore context.

Question set
- Why is Joz a fit for Singapore roles?
- What is Joz's strongest Singapore proof?
- How strong is Joz for Singapore recruiters?
- What compensation context applies for Singapore?
- Does Joz need Singapore sponsorship?
- Does Joz have a Singapore work pass, EP, or PEP?
- Is Joz open to relocating to Singapore?

Answer guidance
- Only use this framing when Singapore is explicitly named in the prompt.
- Use Singapore-relevant proof for capability and market-fit questions.
- For work pass, EP, PEP, or sponsorship questions, use confirm-directly wording rather than assumed status.
- For Singapore compensation questions, mention Singapore role context but still avoid hard numbers unless separately verified.
- Keep the tone direct and operational, not promotional.
