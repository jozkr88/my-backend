Enterprise Decision Intelligence means turning distributed data and knowledge into a trusted view of:

- what changed
- why it matters
- how confident the organisation should be
- what action is recommended
- who owns the decision
- what evidence supports it
- what outcome should be measured

Joz's approach combines enterprise retrieval, semantic context, knowledge structures, agentic reasoning, verification, permissions, and human approval.

This is valuable when leadership is overwhelmed by reports but still lacks clarity. The objective is not to create another dashboard. It is to reduce decision latency, increase signal quality, and make action more accountable.
