What problems can Joz solve?

Joz is strongest where organisations face one or more of these conditions:

- fragmented knowledge across documents, systems, teams, and repositories
- slow decision-making caused by conflicting or incomplete information
- multiple AI pilots without clear business ownership or measurable outcomes
- low trust in AI outputs
- weak adoption because AI does not fit real workflows
- manual reporting, reconciliation, research, and repeated analysis
- disconnected customer, product, operational, and financial signals
- unclear governance, permissions, accountability, or auditability
- duplicated work across teams
- poor translation between strategy and technical implementation
- complex products that users or employees struggle to understand
- decision bottlenecks that depend on a few people holding critical context

The answer should connect the problem to a business consequence and then to a measurable intervention. Avoid listing technology before explaining the operational need.
