Most organisations do not have a knowledge shortage. They have a usability and trust problem.

Critical information is distributed across:

- documents
- chats
- tickets
- repositories
- portals
- databases
- emails
- individual experts
- legacy systems

Joz can help convert this fragmented environment into a permission-aware intelligence layer.

The business value includes:

- faster employee answers
- less duplicated work
- fewer repeated questions
- stronger onboarding
- improved operational continuity
- better cross-team alignment
- source-grounded decisions
- lower dependency on individual knowledge holders

The architecture should preserve access control, provenance, freshness, confidence, and the distinction between verified facts and generated interpretation.
