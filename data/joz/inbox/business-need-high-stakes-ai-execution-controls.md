High-Stakes AI Execution Controls

Purpose
This record explains what matters most when an AI system moves from advice to action in a serious operating environment.

Core idea
The hardest problem is not intelligence. It is safely turning intelligence into action.

What becomes critical
- Security
- Policy enforcement
- Execution controls
- Verification
- Auditability
- Failure recovery
- Real-time state consistency
- Observability

Why this matters
Once an agent can trigger workflows, approvals, transactions, or state changes, the architecture must prioritize controlled execution over demo intelligence. That means deterministic services, permission checks, strong verification, and post-action traceability.

Business value
This is how AI moves from a novelty layer to an operational system that leadership can trust, scale, and govern.
